About Us


Water retention (or edema) is swelling caused by your body holding onto fluids. Your body cannot store caffeine, which means that up to six hours after drinking coffee, caffeine is excreted through increased urine output. This increased output of fluids from the body helps alleviate water retention because of its diuretic effect.
